# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore dataset. The objective is to analyze sales performance, profitability, and regional trends to derive meaningful business insights.

The dashboard focuses on identifying high-performing regions, profitable product categories, and overall sales trends.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel (Data Source)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## KPI Section
The dashboard includes the following key performance indicators:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin %

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

## Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
Shows regional performance comparison to identify top-performing and low-performing regions.

### 2. Sales by Category (Column Chart)
Displays contribution of each product category to total revenue.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
Tracks sales trend over time to identify seasonality patterns.

### 4. Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
Represents distribution of customers across different segments.

---

## Key Insights
- Certain regions contribute significantly higher revenue than others.
- Profit margins vary across product categories.
- Sales show seasonal fluctuations across months.
- Some customer segments generate higher profitability.

---

## Conclusion
The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor overall performance and make data-driven decisions related to regional expansion, category focus, and profitability improvement.
